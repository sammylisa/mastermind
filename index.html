Mastermind

Hover over a peg to select it. To color a selected peg, type the first letter of the color you want.
'b' for blue, 't' for tan, 'w' for white, 'g' for green, 'r' for red, 'y' for yellow, 'o' for orange, 'm' for magenta.
When you are ready to submit your answer for a round, press 'enter'. To clear a peg, click on it. To clear all of the pegs in a round, click the spacebar.
You have 12 rounds to find the right color combo.
